What is SpeakOut?

SpeakOut is a public grievance reporting platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and the authorities meant to serve them. Whether you're facing issues like poor sanitation, broken streetlights, illegal constructions, or public safety concerns — SpeakOut offers a centralized portal where you can raise your voice and ensure your complaint is documented. Every grievance submitted goes into a structured system, making it easier for officials to track, prioritize, and respond effectively.

We believe that transparency and accessibility are key to a more accountable society. That’s why SpeakOut not only empowers users to report issues, but also allows tracking of grievance status, adding comments, and receiving updates. With separate dashboards for users and administrators, the platform ensures smooth communication and better resolution management. SpeakOut isn't just a website — it's a step toward civic engagement, digital empowerment, and social responsibility.
